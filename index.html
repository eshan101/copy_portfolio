My Strategic Process

To ensure consistent results, I follow a proven, four-step process for every project.

1. Deep-Dive & Research

First, I learn your business, your product, and your voice. I analyze your existing data, study your competitors, and most importantly, research your customers to understand what truly motivates them to buy.

2. Strategy & Segmentation

Next, I develop a clear strategy. This involves identifying key customer segments (e.g., new subscribers, repeat buyers, inactive users) and mapping out an email sequence tailored to move each segment toward a specific goal.

3. Copywriting & Psychological Framing

With a clear strategy in place, I write the emails. Each word is chosen to connect with the reader, address their core problems, and position your product as the clear and logical solution.

4. Testing & Optimization

My work isn't done after the emails are written. I build in clear ways to test and measure performance (like different subject lines or calls to action) to ensure your campaigns are continuously improving over time.
